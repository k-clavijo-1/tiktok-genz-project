Y2K Aesthetic

The Y2K aesthetic brings back early 2000s fashion through bold colors, metallic elements, and playful graphics. TikTok has played a major role in reviving this trend through nostalgic and visually engaging content.

Brands have also adapted this aesthetic into their marketing. For example, Canva incorporates Y2K-inspired visuals in its content, showing how brands align with TikTok trends to stay relevant to Gen Z audiences.

Coquette Aesthetic

The coquette aesthetic emphasizes femininity, softness, and romantic visuals such as bows, lace, pastel colors, and delicate makeup. On TikTok, this trend is often expressed through outfit styling and aesthetic-focused content that highlights a dreamy and nostalgic identity.

This trend reflects Gen Z’s interest in self-expression and identity through fashion. Many fashion brands and content creators adopt coquette elements to appeal to audiences who value emotional and visual storytelling.
