Findings

The findings from this project show that TikTok-driven aesthetics play a significant role in shaping how Gen Z perceives brands and makes purchasing decisions. Trends such as the clean girl, coquette, and Y2K aesthetics influence not only individual self-expression, but also how brands present themselves visually and communicate with audiences.

Across the examples analyzed, brands that align with popular TikTok aesthetics appear more relevant, relatable, and engaging to Gen Z consumers. These aesthetics help create a sense of familiarity because they reflect the same styles users see daily on their feeds.

The research also suggests that Gen Z consumers are not only influenced by products, but by the lifestyle and identity associated with those products. When a brand successfully reflects an aesthetic, it can strengthen emotional connection and increase the likelihood of purchase.

  • Visual aesthetics strongly influence Gen Z brand perception.
  • Brands that align with TikTok trends feel more relevant and engaging.
  • Consumers are influenced by the lifestyle and identity behind products.

Conclusion

TikTok has become a major influence on how Gen Z discovers, interprets, and participates in fashion and beauty trends. Aesthetics like clean girl, coquette, and Y2K are more than visual styles. They shape identity, self-expression, and the way young consumers connect with brands online.

Throughout this project, it becomes clear that TikTok trends affect brand perception by making certain brands appear more relevant, aesthetic, and relatable to Gen Z audiences. When brands reflect popular TikTok styles in their visuals, products, or messaging, they are often better able to capture attention and build stronger emotional connections with consumers.

Key Takeaways

  • TikTok helps trends spread quickly and reach large Gen Z audiences.
  • Fashion and beauty aesthetics influence how brands present themselves online.
  • Gen Z is more likely to engage with brands that feel visually current and culturally relevant.

Why This Matters

For brands, understanding TikTok aesthetics is important because Gen Z consumers are highly influenced by online culture and visual identity. Trends move fast, and brands that fail to adapt may seem disconnected from their audience. At the same time, brands must be careful to use these aesthetics in ways that feel authentic rather than forced.

Overall, TikTok continues to shape fashion, beauty, and consumer culture in powerful ways. As trends evolve, brands must pay attention not only to what Gen Z buys, but also to how Gen Z uses aesthetics to express personality, values, and belonging.
