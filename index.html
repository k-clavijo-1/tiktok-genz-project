How TikTok Shapes Fashion & Beauty

TikTok has become one of the most influential platforms in shaping fashion and beauty trends among Gen Z. Through short-form content, aesthetics like the Y2K, Coquette, and Clean Girl styles have gained massive popularity and quickly spread across users and brands.

These trends go beyond visual style—they influence how individuals express identity and how brands position themselves in the market. As a result, TikTok plays a key role in shaping consumer perception and purchasing behavior.

Research Question

How do TikTok-driven fashion and beauty trends influence Gen Z brand perception and purchasing behavior?
